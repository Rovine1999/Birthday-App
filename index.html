AKAN-NAMES WEBSITE

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born.
These "day names" have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person. This naming tradition is shared throughout West
Africa.Most Ghanaians have at least one name from this system, even if they also have an English or Christian name. Notable figures with day
names include Ghana's first president Kwame Nkrumah and former United Nations Secretary-General Kofi Annan.
